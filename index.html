Breaking the Chain of Infections with Corona-Warn-App

This is a simulation of the digital contact tracing using the Corona-Warn-App.
In the simulation pane, the movement of people can be seen. Any person can be selected with a click and their own ID as well as the ones collected locally from people passed are shown.
The ones reported to the RKI and stored on the server are shown too. If a user becomes infected all users who met them are informed and quarantine off the simulation pane.
The parameters of the simulation can be tuned below and the use off the app turned on and off.
Leftmost is the start/stop button for pausing the simulation. To its right the use of the app can be turned on and off.
The amount of people, their speed, distance at which the apps registers a contact as well as the infectiousness of the virus can be tuned with the sliders to the buttons right. Left is the smallest, right the largest value.

Have fun and fiddle around!
